Getting to know Youri: A Personal Journey

Hi, I'm Youri van Deutekom, a 28-year-old allround DTP'er at STYLE CNC Machines. I live with my girlfriend and our beloved cat, creating a warm and comfortable home filled with love and laughter. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new hobbies and interests, and I've been particularly focused on expanding my knowledge in front-end development.

I've always had a passion for design and technology, and that's what led me to my current job at STYLE CNC Machines, where I work as an allround DTP'er. My role involves using my design skills to create visually appealing and functional layouts, as well as my technical expertise to ensure that everything runs smoothly. I take great pride in my work and am always striving to do my best.

In addition to my job, I've been working hard to develop my skills in front-end development, an area that I find particularly fascinating. I believe that a solid understanding of both design and technology is essential for success in today's rapidly changing world, and I'm eager to apply my new skills in new and exciting ways.

Overall, I'm a driven and positive person who values honesty, kindness, and a positive attitude. Whether I'm at work, spending time with my girlfriend, or simply chatting with friends, I'm always looking for ways to bring joy and happiness into the world. I hope this gives you a sense of who I am and what I'm all about.
